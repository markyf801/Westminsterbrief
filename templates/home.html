Why Westminster Brief?

⏱️

Written Questions Scanner

Search the full archive of Parliamentary Written Questions across both Houses by keyword, department, and date range. Instantly see who is asking questions on your policy area, what they are asking, and whether they have been answered. Export a clean list to CSV or a formatted Word document ready for a briefing pack.

Today's PQs Tracker

AI

Your morning briefing tool. Automatically fetches all Written Questions due for answer today — or the most recent sitting day if Parliament is in recess — and uses AI to group them by policy theme. Filter by department, drill into an MP's question history, and download a structured Word briefing in one click. Ideal for daily PQ handling teams.

MP PQ Research

Deep-dive into any MP or Peer's Written Question history. Search by name, filter by department and date, and see every question they have ever tabled — with answered/unanswered status and direct links to Parliament.uk. Useful for understanding a member's policy priorities ahead of a meeting or Select Committee session. Export to Word with one click.

Member Profiles

AI

Generate a comprehensive professional profile for any MP or Peer in seconds. Draws on Parliament's official biography API, registered interests, committee memberships, recent Written Questions, and Wikipedia to produce a structured briefing covering career before parliament, government and opposition roles, policy interests, and key context for meetings. Exports as a formatted Word document with photo.
