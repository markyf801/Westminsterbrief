Written Questions Scanner

Search the full archive of Parliamentary Written Questions across both Houses by keyword, department, and date range. Instantly see who is asking questions on your policy area, what they are asking, and whether they have been answered. Export a clean list to CSV or a formatted Word document ready for a briefing pack.

Today's PQs Tracker

AI

Your morning briefing tool. Automatically fetches all Written Questions due for answer today — or the most recent sitting day if Parliament is in recess — and uses AI to group them by policy theme. Filter by department, drill into an MP's question history, and download a structured Word briefing in one click.

Open Today's PQs Tracker →
🔍

Member Research

Research any MP or Peer in one place. See their career history, government and committee roles, registered interests, recent Hansard speeches, and every Written Question they have tabled — with answered/unanswered status and direct links to Parliament.uk. Useful for preparing ahead of a meeting, select committee session, or stakeholder engagement. Export a combined Word briefing with one click.

Open Member Research →
👤

Member Profiles

AI

Generate a comprehensive professional profile for any MP or Peer in seconds. Draws on Parliament's official biography API, registered interests, committee memberships, recent Written Questions, and Wikipedia to produce a structured briefing covering career, roles, and policy interests. Exports as a formatted Word document with photo.

Open Member Profiles →
🏢

Stakeholder Engagement

BETA

Search thousands of organisations with recorded engagement with Parliament and government — ministerial meetings, select committee evidence, and lobbying register entries. Useful for mapping who is engaging on a policy area ahead of consultations, reviews, or stakeholder meetings. Coverage spans all main UK government departments, 2024–2026.

Open Stakeholder Engagement →

Hansard Search

AI

Search everything said in Parliament on any topic — Commons chamber, Westminster Hall, House of Lords, and Written Answers — simultaneously. AI synthesises results into a structured briefing covering the government position, opposition critique, key speakers, and notable quotes. Also includes Speech Research: analyse a specific minister's debate record.
