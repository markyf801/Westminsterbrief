Westminster Brief

AI-powered parliamentary research tools — built by civil servants, for civil servants.

Why Westminster Brief?

Preparing for parliamentary debates, ministerial meetings, and Select Committee appearances takes significant time. Tracking down relevant Written Questions, reading through Hansard transcripts, researching an MP's background and policy interests, and collating it all into a coherent briefing can take hours — often under tight deadlines.

Westminster Brief brings it all together in one place. Search what has been said in Parliament on any topic, see which questions are due for answer today, build instant profiles of any MP or Peer, and generate formatted Word briefings with a single click — all drawing on live parliamentary data.

Access & Pricing: Westminster Brief is currently free to use for everyone. We may introduce paid tiers in future for private sector and commercial users, but we are committed to keeping full access free for all gov.uk email holders and civil servants in perpetuity. See our Terms of Use for full details.
