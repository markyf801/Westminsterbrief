🧠 How to use the Smart Radar

This system uses Semantic AI Embeddings. It doesn't just look for exact words; it understands the meaning of your search across Parliament, Global News, and Bluesky.

  • Don't use single words: Instead of "funding", use full concepts like "Discussions about universities facing financial bankruptcy or tuition fee caps."
  • The AI filters the noise: The scanner will pull broad news for your chosen department, but the AI will only show you the results that conceptually match your prompt.
  • Stakeholder Tracking: Track specific accounts to see if their public statements align with your policy topics.
